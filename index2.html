General Information

What is it?

It is a system that manages restaurant reservations, it can be used by employees and managers, its purpose is to streamline the reservation management process.

Functionality

In order to ensure user comfort, the forms are optimized in terms of size and buttons, as well as the information displayed.

Main idea

To develop a system to solve different problems that may arise during the management of a reservation in an easy and comfortable way.
